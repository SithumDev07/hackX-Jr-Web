What is hackX Jr?

HackX Jr is an inter-school hackathon which provides a platform for school students to transform their ideas into reality. It promotes school students to build technology-based solutions to problems they see in the society.

Participants are enabled the opportunity to develop their soft skills and find inspiration to their creativity as well as problem-solving capabilities through this competition. It paves the path for school children to be introduced to the IT pathway, create interest and eventually assess the possibilities as well as benefits of IT – based entrepreneurship. It also provides a platform for industries to address the school children, evaluate their potential and benefit from their unbiased and open-minded solutions.

Competition Details

Students from any age group can participate in groups of 5 members and 1 mentor. (A/L students studying ICT from Physical Science stream or Technology stream are highly encouraged to take part)

More than one team can participate from one school.

Idea must be novel and feasible.

A fully functional prototype for the final demonstration is not compulsory, but encouraged.

Proposal and the final pitching can be done in either Sinhala or English medium.

Awareness Session

The awareness session will be conducted prior to the hackathon focusing on several aspects including introduction to hackathons, areas to focus on developing the solutions, technological tools that might be helpful and potential clues on how to market their product.

Lecturers from the Department of Industrial Management, professionals and experts from the related fields will guide teams through brainstorming.

22nd November 2020 from 10.00 a.m. to 12.00 p.m.

via Zoom Platform

Finale

The Final event will require the finalist teams to present their idea or product infront of a judge panel.

(Finalist teams are shortlisted through proposal selection) A prototype is preferred although not compulsory. The evaluation will be done under several criteria such as feasibility, technologies used, marketability, sustainability and adherance to time constraints.

17th December 2020 from 10.00 a.m.

via Zoom Platform

Team Organization

A team should consist of a maximum of five members.

Every group must appoint a mentor for the team, preferably the teacher-in-charge of A/L ICT subject stream, or a teacher of a similar capacity.

No participant is allowed to represent two groups. Inclusion of the same member in two different teams will lead to automatic disqualification of both groups.

Participants are encouraged to design their own promotional material for their team.

Participants can choose an appropriate title for their team.

Awareness Session

An awareness session for the competition will be conducted on 22nd November 2020 from 10.00 a.m. to 12.00 p.m. via Zoom Platform (more details will be informed to registered participants through email, and website).

During this session, the students will be provided with information about the hackathon, areas to focus on developing the solutions, technological tools that might be helpful and potential clues on how to market their product.

Confirmation of the participation for the awareness session is required to be made on or before 20th November 2020 by completing the Awareness Session Registration Form through the www.hackxjr.lk website.

Finale

Final round selected teams will compete on the hackathon, which is scheduled to be held on 17th December 2020 via Zoom Platform.

Each individual team needs to present their idea and how they expect to implement their solution (preferably with a prototype demonstration).

About Us

Vision

Our vision is to be recognized as the pioneering platform for providing the school children of the country with an opportunity to bring forth their ideas and accelerate creation and development of new radical solutions.

Mission

Our mission is to spark innovative ideas and develop a creative and entrepreneurial culture among the school community, who will in turn contribute to global knowledge and economy, by exposing them to the corporate sector.

About Us

HackX Jr is organized by the Industrial Management Science Students' Association (IMSSA), which is the student body of the Department of Industrial Management of the University of Kelaniya.
